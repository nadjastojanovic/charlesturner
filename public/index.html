FAQ

What makes these lessons different from language schools or big platforms?
With one-on-one tutoring, your child gets focused attention every minute of the session. Lessons are tailored to their specific pace, level, and interests, which often means faster progress and more confidence.
Do you offer packages or discounts for multiple lessons?
I offer simple pricing per session, but for long-term students I'm happy to discuss monthly plans or a regular time slot with priority booking.
Do you offer group lessons or only one-on-one?
At the moment, I focus on individual lessons to ensure each student gets my full attention. Group lessons may be possible in the future.
